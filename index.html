Thinking of studying for a postgraduate degree at the University of Chrome?
Let’s Talk Postgrad is your chance to chat with our friendly academics,
giving you the chance to ask questions about our courses to help you make
the right choice for your postgraduate studies.


• find out more about your chosen course, module content and our expert academics
• discover more about our study spaces, delivery methods and what to expect at Herts
• ask about employment prospects, industry connections, accreditations and work placements.
